    OUR VISION

    Delivering tasty and healthy food to people counting on outside food.

    OUR MISSION

    “HAR GHAR STARTUP”: Empowering Chefs to start their kitchens with minimum regulatory and setup hassles.

    OUR VALUES

    3Cs: Compassion: Collaboration: Celebration

    About Us:

    Nibble is the first Mobile Application for Home Food made by genuine chefs. While some may create commercial setups, most of our cooks operate from their home kitchens. We are committed to deliver meals and dishes with Fresh and High Quality Ingredients. Our cooks will ensure that each meal is just as tasty and healthy as the meals they cook for their own family. While commercially prepared food loses the charm of traditional recipes and the magic touch of home cooks, our goal is to delivery gear ka Khana with Char ka Swad. At the same time, we handpick the most delicious dishes offered by the restaurants around you. This ensure no matter which dish you pick, it is a highly rated local favorite.

    We are not open to everyone. Our Chefs are added to our platform only by invitation after going through a selection process by our team. 100% of our Chefs are FSSAI Registered and compliant.

    Nibble enters into an agreement with Home Chefs after our team is satisfied with their operational, organizational and culinary skills.

    We believe in a regular cycle of feedback and as a practice, our team and chefs contact our customers to ensure the best taste and quality is delivered by cooking for individual preferences.

    We wish everyone a Healthy Life. Eat Healthy Home Food and Stay Fit.

    With Gratitude, NIBBLE Team
