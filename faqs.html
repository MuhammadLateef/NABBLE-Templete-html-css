frequently Asked Questions



1. How does the home-made food delivery app work?
- Our app allows you to browse and order delicious homemade meals from local cooks. Simply select your desired dishes, provide delivery details, and enjoy your meal!

2. What types of cuisines are available for delivery?
- Our app offers a wide range of diverse menu of, Indian and international dishes with options for vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals and more. You can explore various options based on your preferences.

3. How do I place an order through the app?
- To place an order, download our app, create an account, browse the available dishes, select your desired items, and proceed to checkout. It's quick and easy!

4. Is there a minimum order requirement?
- The minimum order requirement may vary depending on the cook and location. Please check the app for specific details related to your area.

5. Can I customize my order?
- Yes, you can customize your order by adding special instructions or requesting modifications. Our app provides options to accommodate your preferences.

6. What payment methods are accepted?
- We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and online banking. Choose the option that suits you best during the checkout process.

7. How long does delivery usually take?
- Delivery times can vary based on factors such as distance, traffic, and order volume. Our app provides estimated delivery times for each order, ensuring transparency.

8. Is there a delivery fee?
- Delivery fees may apply depending on the cook and location. The app will display any applicable fees before you confirm your order.

9. Can I track my order?
- Yes, our app offers real-time order tracking. You can monitor the progress of your delivery and receive updates on its status.

10. What if I have dietary restrictions or allergies?
- We understand the importance of dietary restrictions and allergies. Our app allows you to filter dishes based on specific dietary requirements, ensuring you find suitable options.

11. Are there any discounts or promotions available?
- We frequently offer discounts and promotions to our users. Keep an eye on the app's promotions section or subscribe to our newsletter for the latest offers.

12. How can I contact customer support?
- You can reach our customer support team through the app's "Contact Us" section. We are available to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.

13. Can I schedule a future delivery?
- Yes, our app provides the option to schedule future deliveries. Simply select your desired date and time.
